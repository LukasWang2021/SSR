How to compile the project?
1) cd to the root dir of the project.
2) "./make_x86.sh" compile on x86
   "./make_arm.sh" compile on arm-cyclon5
3) output is under "install/lib" and "install/bin" dirs 
4) config files such as xml or yaml are under "install/runtime" dir
5) "./make_clean.sh" delete "output" and "config" dirs
